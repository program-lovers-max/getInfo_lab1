	Travel agency-operated group tours resume for Taiwan residents traveling to mainland: spokesperson
	2023-05-19 12:45
    BEIJING -- A Chinese mainland spokesperson on Friday said that group tours of Taiwan residents to the mainland operated by travel agencies will resume as of this Friday.
    Taiwan compatriots are welcome to visit the mainland for travel and sightseeing, said Ma Xiaoguang, a spokesperson for the Taiwan Affairs Office of the State Council.
