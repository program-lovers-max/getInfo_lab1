	China proposes mechanisms for cooperation with Central Asia: Xi
	2023-05-19 10:30
    XI'AN -- China proposed to establish meeting and dialogue mechanisms in areas including industrial development and investment to facilitate the all-around cooperation between China and Central Asian countries, Chinese President Xi Jinping said Friday.
    Xi made the remarks in his keynote address at the China-Central Asia Summit in Xi'an, Northwest China's Shaanxi province.
    The mechanisms will also cover agriculture, transportation, emergency management, education and political party affairs, according to Xi.
