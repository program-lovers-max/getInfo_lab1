	Britain's cost of living crisis: Farmers
    While inflation in the United Kingdom dropped from 10.1 percent in March to 8.7 percent in April, prices are still rising, even if at a slower rate, and food prices continue to surge.
    As households and small business struggle with high grocery expenses, farmers supplying the food are also facing challenges.
