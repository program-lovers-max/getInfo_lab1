	Zimbabwe's foreign minister lauds China's help
    Frederick Shava, the minister of foreign affairs and international trade of Zimbabwe, told China Daily his country's relations with China are based on solidarity, mutual respect and win-win cooperation. He expressed appreciation to China for the construction of infrastructure projects in Zimbabwe and lauded the Belt and Road Initiative for helping with the development of Africa.
