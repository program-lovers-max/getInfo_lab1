	Tang Paradise brings people back to Tang Dynasty
	2023-05-19 09:26
    Tang Paradise, a renowned scenic spot in Xi'an city of Shaanxi province, is a must-visit for tourists. Designed by expert Zhang Jinqiu, a member of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, it is a cultural theme park built with the Tang Dynasty (618-907) architectural style.
    The park provides visitors with a perfect exhibition of the grandness, prosperity and brilliance of the Tang Dynasty culture and brings visitors back to the prosperous era.
    The welcome ceremony of the China-Central Asia Summit was held in the Tang Paradise on Thursday.
