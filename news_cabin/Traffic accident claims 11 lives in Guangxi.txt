	Traffic accident claims 11 lives in Guangxi
	2023-05-19 13:49
    On Friday morning, eleven people died in a traffic accident in Jingxi city, Guangxi Zhuang autonomous region.
    According to the official WeChat account of Jingxi municipal government, a vehicle carrying 14 people fell off a roadside cliff in Siming village, Yuexu township, Jingxi city. Three people survived and 11 died.
    The rescue and investigation work is underway.
