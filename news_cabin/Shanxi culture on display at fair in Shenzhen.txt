	Shanxi culture on display at fair in Shenzhen
    Shanxi province is putting the charm of its cultural industry on display at the ongoing 19th China (Shenzhen) International Cultural Industries Fair, which kicked off on Wednesday in Shenzhen, Guangzhou province.
    A total of 47 cultural enterprises are presenting more than 30 products, including books, cultural innovation products and food.
    Visitors were attracted by various examples of intangible cultural heritage, including a performance on making flour-based foods and arts and crafts.
