	In Guangzhou, museums mark their day
	2023-05-18 16:39
    Selected museum collections are being showcased at Grandview Mall in Guangzhou, Guangdong province, to mark International Museum Day on Thursday and China Tourism Day on Friday.
    Inheritors of intangible cultural heritage and local artists presented pieces at the event. Visitors also have access to immersive digital adaptions of cultural and museum contents.
    Guangzhou is currently home to 73 museums. More such facilities are in the works.
